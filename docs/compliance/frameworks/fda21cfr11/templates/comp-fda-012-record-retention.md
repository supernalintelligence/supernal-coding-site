# COMP-FDA-012: Record Retention

## Overview

**Purpose**: Electronic record retention system implementation  
**FDA Regulation**: 21 CFR Part 11.10(c) - Record retention  
**Category**: record-management, retention  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: FDA 21 CFR Part 11

## Description

Establishes electronic record retention procedures to ensure records are preserved for required periods and remain accessible, readable, and authentic throughout the retention period.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Record retention policy documented and approved
- [ ] Retention schedule established for all record types
- [ ] Long-term storage and migration procedures implemented
- [ ] Record accessibility and readability verification processes deployed
- [ ] Retention compliance monitoring and reporting systems established
- [ ] Staff training completed on record retention procedures
- [ ] Compliance validation performed and documented

---

_This requirement supports FDA 21 CFR Part 11.10(c) compliance for record retention._
