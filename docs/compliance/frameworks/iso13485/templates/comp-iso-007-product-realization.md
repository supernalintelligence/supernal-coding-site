# COMP-ISO-007: Product Realization

## Overview

**Purpose**: Product realization process implementation  
**ISO 13485 Clause**: 7 - Product realization  
**Category**: product-development, realization  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: ISO 13485

## Description

Establishes product realization processes from planning through delivery, ensuring medical devices meet specified requirements and regulatory compliance.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Product realization procedures documented and approved
- [ ] Product planning and development processes implemented
- [ ] Customer requirements management system deployed
- [ ] Design and development controls established
- [ ] Production and service provision processes implemented
- [ ] Staff training completed on product realization procedures
- [ ] Compliance validation performed and documented

---

_This requirement supports ISO 13485 Clause 7 compliance for product realization._
