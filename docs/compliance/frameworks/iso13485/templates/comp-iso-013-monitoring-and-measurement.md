# COMP-ISO-013: Monitoring and Measurement

## Overview

**Purpose**: Monitoring and measurement system implementation  
**ISO 13485 Clause**: 7.6 - Control of monitoring and measuring equipment  
**Category**: monitoring, calibration, measurement  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: ISO 13485

## Description

Establishes controls for monitoring and measuring equipment to ensure measurement validity and traceability to international standards.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Equipment control procedures documented and approved
- [ ] Calibration and verification programs implemented
- [ ] Equipment identification and records systems established
- [ ] Measurement uncertainty and traceability procedures deployed
- [ ] Equipment maintenance and storage controls implemented
- [ ] Staff training completed on equipment control procedures
- [ ] Compliance validation performed and documented

---

_This requirement supports ISO 13485 Clause 7.6 compliance for control of monitoring and measuring equipment._
