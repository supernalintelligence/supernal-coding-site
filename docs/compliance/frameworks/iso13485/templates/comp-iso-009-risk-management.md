# COMP-ISO-009: Risk Management

## Overview

**Purpose**: Risk management system implementation  
**ISO 13485 Clause**: 7.1 - Planning of product realization (Risk Management)  
**Category**: risk-management, iso14971  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: ISO 13485

## Description

Establishes risk management processes in accordance with ISO 14971 for medical devices throughout the product lifecycle.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Risk management procedures documented per ISO 14971
- [ ] Risk analysis and evaluation processes implemented
- [ ] Risk control measures established and verified
- [ ] Risk management file maintenance procedures deployed
- [ ] Post-market surveillance risk monitoring implemented
- [ ] Staff training completed on risk management procedures
- [ ] Compliance validation performed and documented

---

_This requirement supports ISO 13485 risk management requirements in accordance with ISO 14971._
