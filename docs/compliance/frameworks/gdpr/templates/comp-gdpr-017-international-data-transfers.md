# COMP-GDPR-017: International Data Transfers

## Overview

**Purpose**: International data transfer safeguards implementation  
**GDPR Article**: Chapter V - Transfers of personal data to third countries  
**Category**: data-transfers, international  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: GDPR

## Description

Implements appropriate safeguards for international transfers of personal data to third countries or international organizations, ensuring adequate level of protection.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] International transfer policy documented and approved
- [ ] Transfer impact assessments implemented for all third countries
- [ ] Appropriate safeguards (SCCs, BCRs, adequacy decisions) implemented
- [ ] Transfer monitoring and audit trail systems deployed
- [ ] Data mapping for international transfers completed
- [ ] Staff training completed on international transfer requirements
- [ ] Compliance validation performed and documented

---

_This requirement supports GDPR Chapter V compliance for transfers of personal data to third countries._
