# COMP-GDPR-020: Data Protection Officer

## Overview

**Purpose**: DPO designation and responsibilities  
**GDPR Article**: Articles 37-39 - Data protection officer  
**Category**: governance, oversight  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: GDPR

## Description

Implements designation, independence, and proper functioning of the Data Protection Officer with appropriate resources, expertise, and organizational position.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] DPO designated and registered with supervisory authority
- [ ] DPO independence and autonomy ensured
- [ ] DPO contact details published internally and externally
- [ ] DPO resources and support provided
- [ ] DPO tasks and responsibilities documented
- [ ] Regular DPO reporting to management established
- [ ] DPO training and professional development completed

---

_This requirement supports GDPR Articles 37-39 compliance for data protection officer designation and functioning._
