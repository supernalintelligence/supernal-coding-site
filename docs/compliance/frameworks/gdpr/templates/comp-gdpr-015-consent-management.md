# COMP-GDPR-015: Consent Management

## Overview

**Purpose**: Consent collection and management implementation  
**GDPR Article**: Article 7 - Conditions for consent  
**Category**: consent, legal-basis  
**Priority**: High  
**Framework**: GDPR

## Description

Implements consent management systems ensuring consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous, with mechanisms for withdrawal and record-keeping.

## Definition of Done

- [ ] Consent management policy documented and approved
- [ ] Consent collection interfaces implemented with clear language
- [ ] Consent withdrawal mechanisms deployed and tested
- [ ] Consent records and audit trail systems implemented
- [ ] Granular consent options provided for different processing purposes
- [ ] Staff training completed on consent management procedures
- [ ] Compliance validation performed and documented

---

_This requirement supports GDPR Article 7 compliance for conditions of consent._
